Learning questions

  1. What’s the difference between general relativism and moral relativism?
  2. What’s the difference between moral relativism and moral non-realism?
  3. What are Rachels’s two main arguments against (cultural) moral relativism?
  4. Does Rachels demonstrate that moral relativism is false? What exactly does he show?

Consider

  1. Why might someone be attracted to moral relativism? Can you talk it about it in a way that makes it sound true or compelling?
  2. If relativism isn’t the right way to think about the truth-status of moral or ethical rules, what is the right way to think about that?
